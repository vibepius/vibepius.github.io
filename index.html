Dogs Have Unique Nose Prints, Just like human fingerprints, every dog has a unique nose print. The patterns of ridges and creases on their noses are different for each dog and can be used for identification.

Dogs Have Three Eyelids: Dogs have an upper lid, a lower lid, and a third lid called the nictitating membrane or "haw" which helps keep their eyes moist and protected.
